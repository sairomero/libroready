Frequently Asked Questions

Will my EPUB be accepted by Amazon KDP?

Yes! LibroReady follows all Amazon KDP formatting requirements. We ensure proper chapter structure, table of contents, indentation, and spacing. Our 98% first-time acceptance rate speaks for itself.

What file format do I need to upload?

You need a .docx file (Microsoft Word format). If you have a different format, you can easily convert it to .docx using Google Docs or Microsoft Word.

Do you support books in Spanish?

Yes! LibroReady supports both English and Spanish books. Our chapter detection works with both "Chapter" and "Cap√≠tulo" patterns, plus many other variations.

Can I review the formatting before downloading?

Absolutely! After uploading, you'll see all detected chapters and formatting issues. You can choose which fixes to apply and which chapters to include before generating the final files.

What's included in the Premium plan?

Premium includes everything in Basic plus: AI-powered keyword research (7 optimized keywords for KDP), BISAC category recommendations, an HTML description optimizer, and a simple cover generator. Perfect for authors who want the complete publishing package.

How long does the formatting take?

Most books are analyzed and formatted in under 5 minutes. Larger books (100,000+ words) may take a bit longer, but you'll still have your files in less than 10 minutes.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes! If your formatted EPUB is rejected by Amazon KDP due to formatting issues, we'll fix it for free or provide a full refund. We stand behind our work 100%.
